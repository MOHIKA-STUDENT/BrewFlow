# Future Roadmap — BrewFlow AI

Upcoming engineering features, integrations, and milestones planned for BrewFlow AI.

---

## Milestone 1: Multi-Model AI Routing (Q3 2026)
- **Anthropic Claude Integration:** Support for Claude 3.5 Sonnet to draft longer emails.
- **Local Ollama Integration:** Local model endpoints for secure, offline copy writing.
- **Token Usage Limits:** Custom monthly tokens limit thresholds per workspace organization.

---

## Milestone 2: Automation Webhooks & n8n Hub (Q4 2026)
- **Outbound Webhooks:** Trigger JSON payloads on events (e.g. `lead.created`, `lead.accepted`, `timeline.activity`).
- **Slack & Teams Alerts:** Send workspace logs directly to team channels.
- **n8n / Clay Integrations:** Templates to connect BrewFlow data pipelines directly to cold outreach tools.

---

## Milestone 3: CRM Synchronization (Q1 2027)
- **HubSpot / Salesforce Sync:** Sync leads and notes bi-directionally.
- **Bulk CSV Sourcing:** Drag and drop lists of B2B coordinates to populate the pipeline.
- **Email Client Sync:** Connect Gmail / Outlook inbox threads to the activity timeline feed.
