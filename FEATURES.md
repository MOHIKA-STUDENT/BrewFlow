# Product Features — BrewFlow AI

This document catalogs the functional modules of the BrewFlow AI Sales Operating System.

---

## 1. CRM Lead Directory & Kanban Pipeline
- **Dual View Layout:** Seamlessly toggle between a dense tabular directory (with sorting/filtering) and a visual Kanban Board deck.
- **Drag-and-Drop Lanes:** Drag leads between deal stages ("New Lead", "Contacted", "Interested", "Sample Sent", "Negotiation", "Customer", "Lost") to update database values instantly.
- **Visual Analytics:** Real-time projected revenues are calculated at the top of each Kanban lane.

---

## 2. Outreach AI Assistant
- **Outbox Prompt Drafts:** Contextually drafts emails, WhatsApp pings, cold call hooks, competitor analysis, or follow-up plans using lead context.
- **Interactive Editing Canvas:** Edit generated copy on the fly in the workspace screen.
- **Save Actions:** Approved copy can be committed straight to the lead's database profile notes or timelines with single-click buttons.

---

## 3. B2B AI Lead Scout (Prospect Finder)
- **Logistics Sourcing Engine:** Discover real, pre-verified B2B clients in major regions.
- **ICP Lead Scoring:** Analyzes the target business against your company's value proposition to calculate a match percentage.
- **Scout Sourcing Inbox:** Displays candidates in a temporary workspace dashboard. Click **Accept & Save to CRM** to insert them directly into your database leads table, or **Dismiss** to reject them.

---

## 4. Timeline Interaction Logs
- **Activity Streams:** A chronological history detailing creation logs, state transitions, manual call notes, catalog shipments, or email drafts.
- **Quick Logging Controls:** Log calls, emails, sample packages, or meetings directly from the lead profile banner.
